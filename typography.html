Typography

Headings

If the page has a pager header, do not use the <h1> element. Start with <h2> instead. Heading 3 through 6 are the same size.

Code

<h1>H1 heading (30 pixels)<h1>
<h2>H2 heading (24 pixels)<h2>
<h3>H3 heading (18 pixels)<h3>
<h4>H4 heading (18 pixels)<h4>
<h5>H5 heading (18 pixels)<h5>
<h6>H6 heading (18 pixels)<h6>

Result

H1 heading (30 pixels)

H2 heading (24 pixels)

H3 heading (18 pixels)

H4 heading (18 pixels)

H5 heading (18 pixels)
H6 heading (18 pixels)

Lead Body Copy

A lead body copy is making a sentence or a paragraph stands out among the rest of the contents. To create a lead, apply the lead class to a <p> element.

Code

<p class="lead">Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipisicing elit. Eaque,
nesciunt sapiente eius officiis. Reiciendis, numquam, blanditiis doloribus iste.</p>

Result

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipisicing elit. Eaque, nesciunt sapiente eius officiis. Reiciendis, numquam, blanditiis doloribus iste.

Hyperlink (or Link)

Create a hyperlink between documents or websites by using the <a> element.

Code

<p>Nulla, harum, <a href="http://www.ethanslin.com" target="_blank">Ethan Lin</a>
aut reiciendis eum ipsum soluta sequi dolore cum ratione ex.</p>

Result

Nulla, harum, Ethan Lin aut reiciendis eum ipsum soluta sequi dolore cum ratione.

Blockquote

For quoting blocks of content from another source. Can also be use for quoting a passage from a famous person or literature.

Basic

Code

<blockquote>
	<p>Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipisicing elit.</p>
</blockquote>

Result

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipisicing elit.

With Author or Source

Add an author or a source to the blockqoute by nesting a <small> element after the content.

Code

<blockquote>
	<p>I have not failed. I’ve just found 10,000 ways that won’t work.</p>
	<small>Thomas A. Edison</small>
</blockquote>

Result

I have not failed. I’ve just found 10,000 ways that won’t work.

Thomas A. Edison

Right Aligned

Right aligned the blockquote by applying the pull-right class.

Code

<blockquote class="pull-right">
	<p>When Mr. and Mrs. Dursley woke up on the dull, gray Tuesday our story starts,
	there was nothing about the cloudy sky outside to suggest that strange and
	mysterious things would soon be happening all over the country.</p>
	<small>Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone (page 2)</small>
</blockquote>

Result

When Mr. and Mrs. Dursley woke up on the dull, gray Tuesday our story starts, there was nothing about the cloudy sky outside to suggest that strange and mysterious things would soon be happening all over the country.

Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone (page 2)

Alternative

An alternative blockquote style by applying the quote class. Recommended to use for a long quote.

Code

<blockquote class="quote">
	<p>Here’s to the crazy ones. The misfits. The rebels. The troublemakers. The
	round pegs in the square holes. The ones who see things differently. They’re
	not fond of rules.And they have no respect for the status quo. You can quote
	them, disagree with them, glorify or vilify them. About the only thing you
	can’t do is ignore them. Because they change things. They push the human race
	forward. And while some may see them as the crazy ones, we see genius. Because
	the people who are crazy enough to think they can change the world, are the
	ones who do.</p>
	<small>Steve Jobs</small>
</blockquote>

Result

Here’s to the crazy ones. The misfits. The rebels. The troublemakers. The round pegs in the square holes. The ones who see things differently. They’re not fond of rules. And they have no respect for the status quo. You can quote them, disagree with them, glorify or vilify them. About the only thing you can’t do is ignore them. Because they change things. No Bullets

Create a list of items without bullet style by applying the no-bullet class. All nested <ol> and <ul> elements will not have bullets as well. Works with both <ol> and <ul> elements.

Code

<p>Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipisicing elit. Asperiores,
consectetur laborum ex atque eos possimus doloribus distinctio natus reiciendis
impedit ut earum quasi voluptas delectus molestiae. Laudantium perferendis.</p>
<ul class="no-bullet">
	<li>I'm a bulleted list... OMG where are my bullets!</li>
	<li>My bullets are gone!</li>
		<ol>
			<li>Not even square...</li>
			<li>Not even a number...</li>
		</ol>
	<li>What happened to my bullets!?</li>
	<li>I don't look like a list...</li>
	<li>...incomplete ... an odd ball.</li>
	<li>Use me with cautions...</li>
</ul>

Result

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipisicing elit. Asperiores, consectetur laborum ex atque eos possimus doloribus distinctio natus reiciendis impedit ut earum quasi voluptas delectus molestiae. Laudantium perferendis.
