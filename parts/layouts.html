itaque modi quos delectus nesciunt est facere. Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur
adipisicing elit. Quas, excepturi eius hic officiis at illo natus deserunt necessitatibus

Text Alignments

Code

<p class="center">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.</p>
<p class="right">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.</p>
