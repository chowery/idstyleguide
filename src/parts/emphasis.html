Emphasis

Bold

For emphasizing actions and keywords with a heavier font weight by using the <strong> and <b> elements. It is recommended to use <strong> element rather than <b> element.

Code

<p>The quick brown fox <strong>jumps</strong> over the lazy dog.</p>
<p>The quick brown fox <b>jumps</b> over the lazy dog.</p>

Result

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.

Italics

For emphasizing keywords with a slightly slanted style by using the <em> and <i> elements. It is recommended to use <em> element rather than <i> element.

Code

<p>The quick brown fox jumps over the <em>lazy</em> dog.</p>
<p>The quick brown fox jumps over the <i>lazy</i> dog.</p>

Result

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.

Underline

For emphasizing keywords with an underline by using the <span> element with an underline class. It is not recommended to use <ins> or <u> elements for underlining. These two elements are depreciated in HTML5 by the W3C standards.

Code

<p>The quick brown fox jumps <span class="underline">over</span> the lazy dog.</p>

Result

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.

Abbreviation

Implementation of <abbr> element for abbreviation and acronyms to show the expanded version on hover. The <abbr> element must have a title attribute, which holds the full meaning of the abbreviation or acronyms.

Code

<p><abbr title="HyperText Markup Language">HTML</abbr> is the best thing ever.</p>

Result

HTML is the best thing ever.

Small Text

For de-emphasizing or just a smaller font size for fine print, use the <small> element. <small> element is inline; thus, can be nested inside a <p> element.

Code

<small>The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.</small>

Result

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.

Colored Text

Convey meaning through color by using the following classes: text-info (light-blue), text-success (green), text-warning (yellow), and text-danger (red). Please use these color classes wisely.

Code

<p class="text-info">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.</p>
<p class="text-success">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.</p>
<p class="text-warning">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.</p>
<p class="text-danger">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.</p>

Result

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.
